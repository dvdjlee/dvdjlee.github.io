Introduction

Love the People

Love the People is a café dedicated to serving the people of our community as well as anyone visiting delicious food, beverages, amazing art, and a place to connect with others. Our loving, hard-working staff would love for you to come hang out!

Target audience

Our target audience are people of an age between 15-35 years old. They would visit our site to learn more about our menu, the artists that design the clothing/art offered in our store, and learn more about us and our mission statement.

Their primary tasks and goals would be to eat good breakfast/brunch/lunch food, find a spot to spend quality time with the people thye love, and shop our selection of products.

About

We are located on 1518 Hope St. San Luis Obispo, CA 93405

Open 7:00AM-5PM DAILY

In 2018, Lloyd Drexler had a vision for a cafe that would unite the three things he loves the most; food, art, and people. Growing up, going to his local cafe was his favorite part of week as he would go with his family habitually, every Sunday morning. "The smell of breakfast, comforting interior, and the sound of hustling and bustling of everyone in the cafe was my sanctuary."

Our Artists

We have three amazing artists that we help show off every three months. Love the People is simply a platform to help local artists gain recognition. The profits go to the designer and Krochet Kids, our partner non-profit organization that serves to support, and help women across Africa by providing them work. The women hand sow clothing, which are used for the products available in store.

Lauren Diddy

Lauren is a passionate, minimalist artist that loves creating simple but powerful designs that address prevalent social issues. Aside from designing, she enjoys spending time with her dog, Chico, skiing, and playing soccer.

Paul Martin

Paul is a dynamic artist that is capable of many different artistic styles. His designs stem from pure creativity and joy. In his free time Paul loves travelling, making music, and spending time with his family.

Dave Ploom

Dave is an unorthodox artist that can design wild, mind-consuming designs. When he is not creating art, Dave is surfing, reading, or going to a museum.
